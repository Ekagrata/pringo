Sailica iOS

Mobile IOS application for choosing yachts and marinas by yachtsmen

Mobile Application UX/UI Design

Mobile IOS application for choosing boats and marinas by yachtsmen SAILICA

Sailica mobile application was developed for quick access to useful information. It is essential for yachtsmen to have detailed info about marinas when planning a vacation. This situation usually happens during the yacht trip when there's no pc on a boat. Here, in the app, you can find the phone of the marina, the plan and the radio channel.

https://sailica.com

There is also a meteorological map for the convenience of yachtsmen showing the wind strength and the forecast. The app proposes to book one of 10,000 yachts from the Sailica database.

We developed the back end and the administrative interface and connected the API of third-party services. The application is written with Swift technology. Yachtsmen tell each other about Sailica app and popularize it. The goals set for the team have been achieved.
