Buddha Pizza

Emotional pizza delivery service, different from all the others

Web Application UX/UI Design

WOW and emotional pizza delivery service development

The client came to us with delivery problems. He often got cold, burnt, upside down pizza made from cheap products. To change the situation, he decided to create a new pizza delivery. With such an ambitious plan, “another” should be absolutely everything, including the branding we have created, and then the promotional website.

https://buddhapizza.com

We deliberately made the one-page website with minimum actions and plenty of great animations and illustrations. Just a few clicks and your favourite pizza is in the basket already. We managed to achieve a 'wow' user effect. And after the launch, it simply blew up Facebook: people were sharing and reposting news about new pizzeria and unique website.

When creating a promotional concept, we paid attention to a small bold and witty copywriting. Specific phrases as 'ready to enlightenment', 'zone of influence' and others were provoking emotions in people to remember the website and come again. Another trick was a random order generator called 'Check Your Karma'. No time or desire to think about the order? Our generator will do everything for you. Just enter the number of pizzas and drinks.
